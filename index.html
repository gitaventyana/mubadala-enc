Training & Engagement

Mubadala Group Ethics & Compliance Office provides continuous and ongoing training and engagement across the Mubadala Group for key policy and risk areas to help you understand what is required by the Mubadala Group Code of Ethics and supporting policies.

In addition to the annual acknowledgment that you have read, understood, and will comply with the requirements of the Code, the Ethics & Compliance Office provides training resources and materials that will further educate you on your responsibilities under the Code.

The Ethics & Compliance Helpline is managed by the Ethics & Compliance Office, which will answer your question or look into your concern.

All questions and concerns are handled on a confidential basis and the Ethics & Compliance Office will ensure an appropriate investigation when or if necessary.

All concerns will be tracked with an identification number so those who prefer to remain anonymous can follow up to receive a response or provide more information.
